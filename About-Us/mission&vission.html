YSDF Mission

Our first priority is to make self independent youth. Also aiming to contribute
Atmanirvar Bharat by skilling up the needy people to become self independent.
We are offering Technical, Entrepreneurship, and Management programs which
are / will be in demand by the market leading to self/wage employment.

YSDF Mission

To scale up literacy and create skilled workforce which aligns with the demands
of the employees. To provide excellence in education and leadership in human
development
